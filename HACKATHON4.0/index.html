Code for Social Good

With the rapid advancement of technology, it is important to use its power in ways that benefit society. Hackathon challenges its participants to code for social good, and create an opportunity for us to explore the intersection between technology and society.

On January, over 700 students, hackers, and creators will come together for 48 hours of coding. For the 4th year in a row, we are bringing the most talented students in India to address the state's most pressing issues. Participants can choose to build a project that solves a problem in one of our live themes.

Health

Develop a product that encourages people to live a healthier lifestyle. Help predict and prevent illnesses before they affect the body.
Environment

Aggregate public data to analyze or improve government green energy policies. Design solutions to reduce energy consumption in habitable spaces
Education

Discover new ways to faciliatate learning within the classroom. Enable accessible education for individuals with learning disabilities.
